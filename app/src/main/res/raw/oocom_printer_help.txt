# OOCom Bluetooth Printer Setup

## Quick Setup Guide

1. **Pair your OOCom printer** with your Android device through Bluetooth settings
2. **Open OrtusPOS** and go to Settings > Printers
3. **Select "OCPP M083"** as the printer driver
4. **Tap "Select Bluetooth Printer"** and choose your OOCom printer from the list
5. **Test the connection** using the Device Features screen

## Detailed Instructions

### Step 1: Pair Your Printer
- Turn on your OOCom printer
- Put it in pairing mode (check manual)
- Go to Android Settings > Bluetooth
- Tap your printer when it appears in the list
- Enter PIN if prompted (usually 0000)

### Step 2: Configure in OrtusPOS
- Open OrtusPOS
- Tap the menu button (three lines)
- Tap "Settings"
- Tap "Printers"
- Select "OCPP M083" from the driver dropdown
- Tap "Select Bluetooth Printer"
- Choose your printer from the list

### Step 3: Test Connection
- Go back to the main menu
- Tap "Device Features"
- Tap "OCPP M083 Test"
- Your printer should print a test page

## Troubleshooting

**Printer not found:**
- Make sure it's paired in Android Bluetooth settings
- Restart Bluetooth on both devices
- Try entering the MAC address manually

**Print quality issues:**
- Ensure "OCPP M083" driver is selected (not generic)
- Check printer battery level
- Clean the print head

**Connection problems:**
- Make sure no other app is using the printer
- Forget and re-pair the device
- Restart both devices
